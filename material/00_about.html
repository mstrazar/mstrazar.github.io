You've reached the home page of Martin Stražar, PhD, a researcher in computational biology and machine learning, currently based in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

My main topics of interest include computational method development in microbial genomics, metabolomics, host-microbe interactions and biological sequences (RNA, proteins).

I am an unofficial ambassador of Slovenian Pandolo federation (Zveza društev igre Pandolo Slovenije), a nonprofit custodian of Mediterranean traditional sports.
